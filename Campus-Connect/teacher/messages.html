Question about Assignment 3

Hi Professor,

I have a question about the database design assignment. In the requirements, it mentions that we need to create an ER diagram, but I'm not sure about the level of detail expected.

Specifically:

  • Should we include all attributes for each entity?
  • Do we need to show cardinality for all relationships?
  • Are weak entities required for this assignment?

I want to make sure I'm on the right track before I submit. Thank you for your time!

Best regards,

Hi John,

Great questions! Here are the answers to your questions about the ER diagram:

  1. Attributes: Yes, please include all major attributes for each entity. You don't need to include every possible attribute, but focus on the key ones that are relevant to the business requirements.
  2. Cardinality: Absolutely! Showing cardinality (1:1, 1:M, M:N) is essential for understanding the relationships between entities.
  3. Weak entities: Only if they naturally arise from your design. Don't force them, but if you identify entities that depend on others for their existence, please include them.

I've also uploaded a sample ER diagram to the course materials that might help guide you. Feel free to come to office hours if you need further clarification.

Best,
Professor
